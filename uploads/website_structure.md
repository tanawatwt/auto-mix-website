## HOME PAGE

- Supporting text

---

### Highlight Services
- Grid of service cards
- Each card includes:
  - Service name
  - Short description
  - Icon or image
  - Link to service page

---

### Expertise / Knowledge Section
Purpose: show that the client is not only a service provider, but also an expert in their field.

Content ideas:
- Short educational tips
- Common customer problems and solutions
- Before-service checklist
- Professional recommendations
- Industry-specific knowledge

For AUTO MIX example:
- “How to choose the right engine oil for your car”
- “When should you check your suspension?”
- “Why transmission flushing matters”
- “Signs your brake system needs inspection”

Layout:
- 3–4 cards
- Each card includes:
  - Topic title
  - Short expert explanation
  - Icon or image
  - Link to related blog post

---

### Blog Preview Section
Purpose: bring educational content into the home page and improve SEO.

Section title examples:
- Latest Articles
- Expert Tips
- Knowledge from Our Team
- Car Care Tips

Each blog preview card includes:
- Featured image
- Blog category
- Title
- Short excerpt
- Publish date
- Read more link

Recommended display:
- 3 latest posts
- 1 featured post + 2 smaller posts
- Link to full Blog page

For AUTO MIX example posts:
1. How to choose the right engine oil for European and Asian cars
2. 5 signs your suspension should be checked
3. Why brake fluid flushing is important for safety

## BLOG PAGE

### Purpose
The Blog page should demonstrate the expertise of the client’s business, build trust with customers, answer common questions, and support SEO.

### Blog Categories
Use categories based on the client’s industry.

For AUTO MIX example:
- Engine Oil & Fluids
- Brake System
- Suspension
- Battery
- Car Care Tips
- Product Knowledge
- Travel / Pre-trip Inspection
- Promotions & News

### Blog Listing Layout
Each post includes:
- Featured image
- Category
- Title
- Short excerpt
- Publish date
- Read more link

Recommended layout:
- Featured article at top
- Category filter
- Search bar
- Blog card grid
- CTA banner after several posts

**Category:** Engine Oil & Fluids

## CONTENT STRATEGY FOR BLOGS

Each blog post should be connected to the client’s real expertise.

Recommended blog types:
- How-to guides
- Common problem explanations
- Product recommendations
- Maintenance checklists
- Seasonal preparation tips
- Case studies from real work
- Promotion explanation posts

For service businesses, blog content should:
- Answer real customer questions
- Explain why the service matters
- Show the business knows what it is doing
- Link back to the relevant service page
- End with a clear contact or booking CTA
