# AUTO MIX – Website Design System Prompt

## 1. Brand Overview
AUTO MIX is a modern automotive service center specializing in:
- Engine oil & fluids
- Brake systems & suspension
- Headlight installation
- Automotive products (online & in-store)

The brand tone is:
- Professional
- High-performance
- Trustworthy
- Modern automotive-tech

---

## 2. Visual Identity

### Logo Usage
- Primary logo: bold "AUTO MIX" in yellow
- Background: dark/black
- Use horizontal logo for navbar
- Maintain clear space around logo (min 1x height of letter "A")

### Color Palette

#### Primary Colors
- **Auto Yellow**: `#FFC107` (main highlight color)
- **Deep Black**: `#0D0D0D` (background)
- **Charcoal Dark**: `#1A1A1A` (secondary background)

#### Secondary Colors
- **Accent Orange**: `#FF6A00`
- **Steel Gray**: `#A0A0A0`
- **White**: `#FFFFFF`

#### Usage Rules
- Dark UI base (black/charcoal)
- Yellow for CTAs, highlights, icons
- Orange for subtle accents/hover states

---

## 3. Typography

### Headings
- Font: **Poppins / Montserrat / Kanit (Thai support)**
- Style: Bold, uppercase
- Tracking: Slightly expanded

### Body Text
- Font: **Inter / Prompt / Kanit**
- Weight: Regular / Medium
- Color: White or light gray

### Hierarchy
- H1: Large, bold (hero sections)
- H2: Section titles
- H3: Cards/services
- Body: 16–18px readable

---

## 4. Layout & Grid

### Structure
- Max width: 1200–1400px
- Grid: 12-column responsive
- Sections spaced with generous padding (80–120px)

### Sections
1. Hero (cover image with service highlights)
2. Services overview
3. Brands/products (ZIC, Liqui Moly, Motul, etc.)
4. Workshop gallery
5. Promotions / posts
6. Contact & location

---

## 5. UI Components

### Buttons
- Primary: Yellow background, black text
- Hover: Darker yellow / orange glow
- Shape: Slightly rounded (8px)

### Cards
- Dark background
- Subtle shadow or glow
- Yellow accent line or icon

### Icons
- Automotive-related icons
- Use line or solid style in yellow/white

---

## 6. Imagery Style

### Image Types
- Automotive service photos
- Workshop environment
- Mechanics working
- Product displays

### Style
- High contrast
- Slight dark overlay for readability
- Yellow highlights or lighting accents

---

## 8. UX Principles

- Fast and mobile-first
- Clear CTAs always visible
- Easy contact (sticky call / Line button)
- Trust-focused (real images > stock)

---

## 9. Animation & Effects

- Subtle hover animations
- Glow effects on yellow elements
- Smooth scroll transitions
- Avoid over-animation

---

## 10. Tone of Voice

- Confident and professional
- Simple and direct
- Automotive-focused
- Bilingual support (Thai + English optional)

---

## 11. Technical Recommendations

- Framework: Next.js / React / Vue
- Styling: Tailwind CSS preferred
- Optimize images for performance
- SEO-friendly structure

---

## 12. Deliverable Expectations

The final website should:
- Match AUTO MIX branding exactly
- Feel modern and premium
- Be conversion-focused (calls, bookings)
- Work perfectly on mobile
