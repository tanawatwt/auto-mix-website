# AUTO MIX – Client Information for Website

## 1. Business Profile

**Business Name:** AUTO MIX / Auto Mix / ออโต้มิกซ์  
**Business Type:** Automotive service center and automotive product retailer  
**Location:** Nakhon Ratchasima, Thailand  
**Facebook Audience:** 4.1K followers  
**Main Positioning:** Standard automotive service center for European and Asian cars.

AUTO MIX provides professional vehicle maintenance services including engine oil changes, automotive fluids, suspension work, battery replacement, transmission flushing, gear filter replacement, brake services, and car care products. The shop focuses on high-quality global-standard products, experienced technicians, honest recommendations, and transparent pricing.
